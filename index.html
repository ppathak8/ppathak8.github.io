Recently, I finished my doctoral studies at Subaru Telescope, under the supervision of Prof. Olivier Guyon.

Currently I am working on one of the modules of SCExAO instrument, which is a high-contrast imaging instrument for direct detection of exoplanets at Subaru telescope. My current research involves active on-sky closed-loop correction of residual atmospheric dispersion for high contrast coronagraphy.
